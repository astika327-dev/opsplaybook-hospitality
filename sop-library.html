Housekeeping — Room Inspection

Ensure rooms meet operational quality and guest expectations.

  • Doors, locks, and handles inspected
  • Bed linen clean and properly arranged
  • Amenities replaced (soap, shampoo, tissue)
  • Bathroom cleaned & disinfected
  • AC and lights functional
  • Floor swept, mopped, and vacuumed
  • Trash bins emptied

Laundry — Linen Handling

Prevent loss and maintain hygiene for sheets, towels, and pillowcases.

  • Sort by fabric and soil level
  • Use recommended detergent & temperature
  • Track counts in/out

Front Office — Check-in
