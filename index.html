Housekeeping Room Inspection

Standard procedures for ensuring rooms meet operational quality and guest expectations.

  • Doors, locks, and handles inspected
  • Bed linen clean and properly arranged
  • Amenities replaced (soap, shampoo, tissue)
  • Bathroom cleaned & disinfected
  • Air conditioning and lights functional
  • Floor swept, mopped, and vacuumed
  • Trash bins emptied
